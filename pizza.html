PIZZA AND PASTA

Located near the South Beach, Carinis Pizza and Pasta comes as a local, straightforward eatery for the people living in Durban. Providing the authentic taste of Italy, the restaurant is an informal eatery having large windows that provide patrons a beautiful outside view while dining. Its spiffy brick interior gives the restaurant a classic look of those decade old buildings in Italy.

Carini's Pizza and Pasta offers a lunch and a dinner menu. Apart from pizzas and pastas, the restaurants menu includes salad, cold and hot sandwiches, veal, chicken and seafood dishes, calzones and desserts. Some of its popular pizza flavours are barbeque chicken and Philly cheese steak. Some of the other popular dishes include Zuppa de Pesce, veggie and meat lasagne and ricotta balls. Carini's Pizza and Pasta is open the entire week from 11:30 a.m. to 10:00 p.m
